The Highlights ✨

Ten days, a boat through a wine-country canyon, open-air concerts, and as little forced fun as possible.

🚤

Ribeira Sacra

Miño river boat trip (Belesar), Cabo do Mundo, vineyard terraces. Boat and food kept separate — dinner after at A Faragulla, Chantada.

Book ahead
🌲

Fragas do Eume

Atlantic Europe's best-preserved coastal forest. Note: road to the monastery is car-free in summer, check the shuttle situation first.

Free entry
⛵

Baiona

Coastal town near Vigo. Replica of the Pinta in the harbour, a fortress-headland walk. Cooler than inland Ourense in August.

Free to wander
🎶

Semana Clásica

Free open-air orchestral concerts in Plaza de María Pita, most evenings 18–23 Aug.

Free, just show up early
🎤

Judeline

As Noites do Parque, Parque Santa Margarita, 26 August evening.

Ticketed
🏖️

Beach days

Skipping Riazor/Orzán crowds for Bastiagueiro, Ares, Doniños or Frouxeira instead.

Free
📸

MOP Foundation

Free fashion-photography exhibitions in a converted port silo, calm and self-paced.

Free entry

24 MonBaiona›
Coastal town near Vigo, roughly 1hr45min–2hrs each way. Walkable old town, a full-size replica of the Pinta moored in the harbour (the ship that first brought news of Columbus's return to Europe), and a parador built into a medieval fortress on a headland with a coastal walk around it. Swapped in for Ourense, inland heat in August was a real concern, Baiona runs cooler being on the coast.
